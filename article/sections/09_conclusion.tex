\section{Conclusion}

This manuscript scaffold turns the current \texttt{COCOON-Sionna} repository into a paper-ready IEEE structure for a Cocoon concept-plus-simulation study. The draft already separates motivation, scope, system model, simulation setup, geometry results, \gls{sinr} results, and relocation results, while keeping all figure references tied to centralized path macros.

The immediate next step is to replace the placeholder interpretation text with the first stable Rabot result set, freeze the concept figure, and populate the strategy summary table from the committed postprocessing artifacts. After that, the paper can expand toward richer scenario comparisons, coverage maps, and eventually the measurement and proof-of-concept layers envisaged in the broader Cocoon program.
